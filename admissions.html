FAQs

Quick answers about grades, medium, admissions, and getting started.

Which grades do you offer?
We offer classes from Nursery to Grade 10, with strong foundational learning and steady academic growth.
Is the school English-medium?
Yes. English is the medium of instruction, and we also support Hindi for clear understanding and confidence.
Is it co-educational?
Yes, we are co-educational and welcome both boys and girls in a safe, respectful environment.
How do we start the admission process?
Fill the Enroll Today form. Our team will contact you with the next steps, document checklist, and a visit schedule.
